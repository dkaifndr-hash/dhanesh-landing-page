Questions Leaders Ask

How is this different from hiring a traditional consulting firm?

Traditional firms deliver slide decks and leave. I partner with your leaders and teams throughout the journey — coaching, mentoring, and ensuring adoption becomes voluntary and sustainable. My focus is on people, not just processes.

How long before we see results?

Leadership alignment and early team improvements typically emerge within 4-6 weeks. Enterprise-wide cultural transformation takes 6-12 months of sustained partnership — but the momentum builds quickly.

Do you work with remote or offshore teams?

Yes. I have extensive experience leading transformations across distributed, remote, and offshore teams. I design approaches that create connection and alignment regardless of location.

What frameworks do you use?

I'm certified in SAFe®, LeSS, Kanban, Scrum, and Lean. But I don't prescribe a framework first — I assess your organization's reality and apply what fits. Sometimes the best approach is a custom blend.

What does the free discovery call cover?

A 15-30 minute conversation to understand your transformation goals, discuss current challenges, and explore practical next steps. No pitch, no obligation — just a diagnostic and honest guidance.

What's your engagement model?

I offer advisory (strategy), coaching (embedded engagement), implementation (SAFe® deployment), and training (workshops). Most clients start with a discovery call, then move to a 3-month pilot before committing to a longer engagement.
