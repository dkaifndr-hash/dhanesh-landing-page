Questions Leaders Ask

What services does Pragility offer?

We provide enterprise Agile coaching, mentoring for leaders and Scrum Masters, SAFe® certifications, Scrum Master and Product Owner training, and tailored workshops and seminars to help organizations thrive.

How can Agile coaching benefit my organization?

Agile coaching helps organizations improve delivery speed (typically 30-40% faster), increase team alignment and morale, reduce wasted work, and build a culture of continuous improvement. It's particularly valuable for enterprises struggling with scaling, silos, or transformations that have stalled.

What is SAFe certification?

SAFe® (Scaled Agile Framework) certification trains individuals to lead Agile transformations at enterprise scale. We offer SAFe® Agilist (SA), Practitioner (SP), Scrum Master (SSM), and Advanced Scrum Master (SASM) certifications. These are the industry standard for organizations scaling Agile across multiple teams.

Who are the ideal candidates for Agile coaching?

Our coaching is designed for C-suite executives, VPs, Directors, Agile Coaches, Scrum Masters, Product Owners, and teams undergoing Agile transformation. We work with anyone responsible for driving change or improving delivery in an enterprise setting.

How long does the coaching engagement last?

Most engagements start with a 3-month pilot to assess needs and demonstrate value. Longer engagements typically run 6-12 months for full transformation. Training programs range from 2-day workshops to multi-week certification courses. We tailor the duration to your organization's specific goals.

Can Pragility assist with remote or distributed teams?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience leading transformations across distributed, remote, and offshore teams. Our coaching approach is designed to create connection and alignment regardless of location, using asynchronous and synchronous methods.
