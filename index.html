Questions Leaders Ask

What services does Pragility offer?

We provide enterprise Agile coaching, mentoring for leaders and Scrum Masters, SAFe certifications, and tailored workshops to help organizations thrive.

How can Agile coaching benefit my organization?

Agile coaching helps organizations improve delivery speed (typically 30-40% faster), increase team alignment, reduce wasted work, and build a culture of continuous improvement.

What is SAFe certification?

SAFe certification trains individuals to lead Agile transformations at enterprise scale. We offer SAFe Agilist, Practitioner, Scrum Master, and Advanced Scrum Master certifications.

How long does the coaching engagement last?

Most engagements start with a 3-month pilot, then run 6-12 months for full transformation. Training programs range from 2-day workshops to multi-week certification courses.

Can Pragility assist with remote or distributed teams?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience leading transformations across distributed, remote, and offshore teams using both asynchronous and synchronous methods.
