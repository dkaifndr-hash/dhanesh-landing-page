Our Security Commitment

At Pragmatic Agility, we take the security of your data seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and ensure the integrity of our services. Our security practices are designed to meet or exceed applicable regulatory requirements and industry best practices.

Data Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2/1.3 (Transport Layer Security). This ensures that your data cannot be intercepted or read by unauthorized parties during transmission. Our SSL certificate is maintained and renewed regularly to ensure continuous protection.

Infrastructure Security

Our website and services are hosted on secure, enterprise-grade infrastructure with the following protections:

  • DDoS Protection: Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) mitigation to ensure service availability
  • Web Application Firewall (WAF): Protection against common web vulnerabilities including SQL injection, cross-site scripting (XSS), and other OWASP Top 10 threats
  • Regular Security Updates: All software components are kept up to date with the latest security patches
  • Intrusion Detection: Automated monitoring for suspicious activity and potential security breaches
  • Backup and Recovery: Regular backups with tested recovery procedures to ensure data integrity

Access Controls

We implement strict access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel can access your data:

  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all administrative access
  • Least Privilege: Access is granted on a need-to-know basis with minimum necessary permissions
  • Audit Logging: All access to sensitive data is logged and monitored
  • Regular Access Reviews: Periodic review and revocation of unnecessary access rights

Data Protection

We employ multiple layers of data protection:

  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data is encrypted when stored
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the information necessary to provide our services
  • Retention Limits: Data is retained only as long as necessary and securely deleted when no longer needed
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

Form Security

Our lead capture and assessment forms include multiple security measures:

  • Input Validation: All form inputs are validated to prevent injection attacks
  • Email Verification: Work email required; personal email domains are blocked to ensure legitimate business inquiries
  • Profanity Filtering: Content filtering to prevent abuse
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against automated form submissions
  • No Data Storage: Assessment data is processed in memory and not permanently stored on our servers

Security Headers

Our website implements the following HTTP security headers:

  • X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff — Prevents MIME type sniffing
  • X-Frame-Options: DENY — Prevents clickjacking attacks
  • Referrer-Policy: strict-origin-when-cross-origin — Controls referrer information
  • Strict-Transport-Security — Enforces HTTPS connections
  • Content-Security-Policy — Prevents XSS and data injection attacks

Incident Response

In the event of a security incident, we have a defined incident response plan that includes:

  • Immediate containment and assessment of the incident
  • Notification to affected users within 72 hours, as required by applicable regulations
  • Investigation and remediation of the root cause
  • Implementation of measures to prevent similar incidents in the future

Compliance

Our security practices are designed to comply with applicable data protection regulations, including:

  • PIPEDA — Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (Canada)
  • GDPR — General Data Protection Regulation (EU), where applicable
  • CASL — Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation

Report a Security Issue

If you discover a security vulnerability or have concerns about our security practices, please contact us immediately at info@pragility.ca. We take all security reports seriously and will respond promptly.
